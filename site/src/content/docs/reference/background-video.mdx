Decorative background video that automatically plays muted and looped. Renders a `<video>` inside a shadow DOM positioned to fill its container. By default, the video is muted, looped, and autoplaying — use the opt-out attributes below to change this behavior.

## Anatomy

<FrameworkCase frameworks={["html"]}>
```html
<background-video src="video.mp4"></background-video>
```
</FrameworkCase>

<FrameworkCase frameworks={["react"]}>
```tsx
<BackgroundVideo src="video.mp4" />
```
</FrameworkCase>

      <Td><MarkdownCode>nomuted</MarkdownCode></Td>

    </Tr>
    <Tr>
      <Td><MarkdownCode>noloop</MarkdownCode></Td>

    </Tr>
    <Tr>
      <Td><MarkdownCode>noautoplay</MarkdownCode></Td>
      <Td>Opt out of autoplay. By default the video autoplays.</Td>
    </Tr>
  </Tbody>
